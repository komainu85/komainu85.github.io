Very quick post post, I know this has been catching a number of people out. Such as this Stack Overflow question. http://stackoverflow.com/questions/33020451/sitecore-speak-and-javascript/33023142#33023142

When debugging Sitecore SPEAK in Sitecore 7.x (query string ?sc_debug=1)  you could access and debug your SPEAK application in your console browser via Sitecore.app. When trying this in Sitecore 7.5 and 8 you will get "undefined" returned.

In Sitecore 7.5 and 8.x Sitecore.app has been moved. The new namespace is Sitecore.Speak.app.
